About Star Wars Operations

Star Wars Operations is an immersive online strategy game that puts you in command of iconic forces from the Star Wars universe. Whether you choose to lead the disciplined ranks of the Galactic Empire or fight for freedom with the Rebel Alliance, every decision shapes the fate of the galaxy.

Experience epic space battles, manage resources across multiple star systems, and deploy legendary characters like Darth Vader, Luke Skywalker, and Princess Leia in tactical combat scenarios. The game combines real-time strategy elements with turn-based planning, creating a unique gameplay experience that captures the essence of the Star Wars saga.

Built for the modern web, Star Wars Operations runs seamlessly in your browser without requiring downloads or installations. Join millions of players in this epic galactic conflict and prove your strategic mastery in battles that will determine the future of the galaxy far, far away.

Strategic Gameplay

Success in Star Wars Operations requires mastering both military strategy and resource management. Choose your faction wisely - the Empire excels in raw firepower and technological superiority, while the Rebellion relies on guerrilla tactics, speed, and adaptability.

Resource management is crucial to victory. Mine precious metals from asteroid fields, establish trade routes between friendly systems, and invest in research to unlock advanced technologies. Balance your economy between military expansion and infrastructure development to maintain long-term strategic advantage.

Combat strategy varies significantly between space and ground operations. In space battles, positioning and fleet composition determine victory, while ground assaults require careful coordination of infantry, vehicles, and air support. Master both domains to achieve total galactic dominance.

Choose Your Path

The galaxy awaits your command. Will you restore order as the Galactic Empire, using fear and superior technology to maintain peace through strength? The Empire offers powerful Star Destroyers, advanced TIE fighter squadrons, and the might of the Dark Side through Sith Lords.

Or will you fight for freedom with the Rebel Alliance, using cunning tactics and hope to overcome overwhelming odds? The Rebellion provides versatile X-wing fighters, skilled pilots, and the power of the Force through Jedi Knights who refuse to surrender to tyranny.

Each faction offers unique gameplay experiences, special units, and distinct strategic advantages. Your choice will shape not only your tactical options but also the story campaigns available to you, providing hours of engaging single-player content alongside competitive multiplayer battles.

Getting Started

Begin your journey by selecting your faction and completing the tutorial missions. These introductory scenarios teach essential gameplay mechanics while immersing you in classic Star Wars battles. Learn to manage your economy, build military units, and engage in both space and planetary combat.

Start with smaller skirmishes to master the controls and strategic concepts before attempting larger campaign battles. Pay attention to unit strengths and weaknesses - TIE fighters excel against other starfighters but are vulnerable to capital ship weapons, while ground vehicles dominate infantry but struggle against air units.

Remember that victory often depends on adaptation and tactical flexibility. The best commanders can adjust their strategy mid-battle, exploiting enemy weaknesses while protecting their own vulnerable units. May the Force be with you, young commander!
